About

Genius Annotation

Jon Anderson, the songwriter, is a very spiritual person, and (therefore) strongly opposed to unjust war, like the one in Vietnam at the time of this song.

The song is against the war and encourages people to reach out spiritually, to be in touch with themselves and goodness/god.
